What is Baby Bits?

Baby Bits is a mobile application that helps new mothers by allowing friends and family to chip in with childcare tasks. It provides an organized platform for parents to share the responsibilities of motherhood - such as watching the newborn, grocery shopping, doing laundry and more. With Baby Bits, everyone can easily contribute.

Why use Baby Bits?

Being a new mother can be overwhelming, and it's important to have a support system to help with the added responsibilities. Baby Bits makes it easy for friends and family to pitch in and share the workload. With Baby Bits, you can focus on taking care of yourself and your newborn, knowing that others are helping out too.

How does it work?

To use Baby Bits, simply download the app and create an account. Then, you can create tasks for others to help with and assign them to friends and family. You can also view and claim tasks that have been created by others. It's a simple and easy way to share the workload of motherhood.
